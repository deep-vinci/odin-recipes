Panipuri

Description

  • 20 puris (hollow round shells made of semolina or wheat flour)
  • 1 cup tamarind chutney
  • 1 cup green chutney
  • 2 cups boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup boiled chickpeas
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon chaat masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon roasted cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups spicy tangy water (prepared by mixing tamarind chutney, green chutney, chaat masala, roasted cumin powder, and salt in water)

Steps

  1. Make a hole in the center of each puri using your thumb or a small spoon.
  2. Fill each puri with a small amount of mashed potatoes and chickpeas.
  3. Top with chopped onion, tomato, and coriander leaves.
  4. Sprinkle chaat masala and roasted cumin powder over the fillings.
  5. Dip each filled puri in the spicy tangy water.
  6. Place the puris on a serving plate and serve immediately.
  7. Enjoy the burst of flavors in your mouth!
